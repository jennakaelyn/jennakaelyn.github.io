Music is a wonderful thing, isn't it?

So wonderful in the sense that music can take you places. For example, I'm sitting in this cafe right now and they just started playing Meghan Trainor, so now I'm leaving to a different cafe.

The best work comes from the heart

There's absolutely no doubt about it. Someone who loves what they do will go to the moon and back to perfect their work. And with nothing but eager will to do so.

Take Casey Neistat for example. Sure, he's one of the most hyped up celebrities out there, but it's all warranted. I promise.

Someone who takes that much time into getting the perfect angles and making every shot count is someone who has fallen deeply in love with his work, and that's the type of person I aspire to be. I mean, come on. Just watch this video.
